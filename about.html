About

This is the blog of Stephen Haberman.

After years of thinking I did not have much to contribute, my ego has finally convinced me otherwise (actually in retrospect I like writing things up because it leads to more clarity of that, and it's just kind of fun). Whether or not this is true remains to be seen.

I was a co-founder of Exigence (wow that was a long time ago), then enjoyed working for Bizo (then LinkedIn), a too-short stint at Remind and now Homebound.

I enjoy software development a little too much. It makes me good at what I do, but it also brings out some of my draconian tendencies.

I'm on github and LinkedIn. I have a handful of open source projects, but Joist and Tessell are were the primary ones.
